colorpoolα

colorpool is a color manipulation library written in PHP. It lets you easily convert RGB color values to more perception-friendly color spaces such as HSL and HSV and vice-versa. It helps you easily find contrasting, analogous, monochrome colors.

Lab color spaces, helpers for generating palettes a la kuler.
